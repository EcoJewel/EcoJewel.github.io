Community

Join a global network of environmental advocates

Collective Environmental Responsibility

Beyond individual empowerment, EcoJewel fosters a sense of collective environmental responsibility through community engagement features. Users can seamlessly share their air quality data and experiences, creating a collaborative ecosystem that contributes to a comprehensive environmental dataset and catalyzes grassroots initiatives and campaigns.

Global Environmental Mapping

See real-time aggregated air quality data from EcoJewel users around the world. Each dot represents environmental measurements contributed by our community members, creating a comprehensive picture of global air quality.

This interactive map shows CO2 levels and environmental data points collected by our community. Green indicates healthy air quality, yellow shows moderate levels, orange represents elevated levels, and red indicates areas of concern.

Community Features

DATA

Data Sharing

Choose to share your environmental data anonymously to contribute to a global dataset. Your contributions help researchers and policymakers understand air quality patterns.

AWARDS

Challenges & Achievements

Participate in community challenges to reduce carbon footprints. Earn badges and recognition for making environmentally conscious decisions.

FORUM

Discussion Forums

Connect with other EcoJewel users to share tips, ask questions, and discuss environmental topics. Learn from others' experiences and insights.

LOCAL

Local Groups

Find and join local EcoJewel communities in your area. Organize meetups, environmental initiatives, and collaborative projects.

SHARE

Social Sharing

Share your environmental journey on social media. Inspire friends and family to become more aware of their environmental impact.

LEARN

Educational Resources

Access a library of educational content about air quality, climate change, and environmental stewardship created by and for the community.

Community Guidelines

We're committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and supportive community. All members are expected to:

  • Share data responsibly and respect others' privacy
  • Engage in constructive and respectful discussions
  • Support fellow community members in their environmental journey
  • Base discussions and recommendations on scientific evidence
  • Report any concerns or inappropriate behavior to moderators
