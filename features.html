Features

Comprehensive environmental monitoring at your fingertips

Real-time CO2 Tracking

EcoJewel's built-in air quality sensor measures CO2 levels in your surroundings, helping you understand your personal exposure to air quality both indoors and out.

  • Continuous monitoring of carbon dioxide levels
  • Measurements displayed in parts per million (ppm)
  • Instant alerts when CO2 levels exceed healthy thresholds
  • Historical data tracking to identify patterns
  • Accurate readings within seconds of environmental changes

LED Air-Quality Indicator

Visualize the invisible. A discreet LED embedded in the necklace shifts color in real time based on CO2 levels, so you can react immediately without opening the app.

  • Green - Healthy levels (400-700 ppm)
  • Yellow - Moderate levels (700-1000 ppm)
  • Orange - Elevated levels (1000-1500 ppm)
  • Red - High levels (1500+ ppm)

No need to check your phone - the LED provides instant visual feedback on air quality.

3D Printed, Sustainable Casing

Eco-friendly and planet-conscious, the casing uses 3D-printed materials to minimize manufacturing waste and support sustainable design without compromising elegance.

  • Biodegradable PLA filament from renewable resources
  • Customizable designs and colors
  • Lightweight and comfortable for all-day wear
  • Minimizes traditional manufacturing waste
  • Durable and water-resistant construction

Comprehensive Environmental Sensors

Beyond CO2, EcoJewel monitors a complete range of environmental factors:

  • Temperature - Track ambient and apparent temperature
  • Humidity - Monitor moisture levels in your environment
  • Air Pressure - Understand weather patterns
  • Wind Data - Get wind speed and direction information
  • Visibility - Track atmospheric clarity

Personalized Insights & Recommendations

The EcoJewel app processes your environmental data and generates personalized insights based on your activities and preferences:

  • Custom CO2 threshold settings based on your sensitivity
  • Recommendations for improving indoor air quality
  • Suggestions for optimal times to ventilate your space
  • Activity-based insights (e.g., exercise, sleep, work)
  • Long-term trends and pattern analysis

Bluetooth Low Energy Connectivity

Seamlessly sync your EcoJewel necklace with your smartphone:

  • Energy-efficient Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology
  • Extended battery life - up to 7 days on a single charge
  • Automatic syncing when in range
  • Compatible with iOS and Android devices
  • USB-C charging port for convenience
